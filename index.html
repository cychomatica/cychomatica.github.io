About me

I am Shutong Wu (吴枢同), a first-year Ph.D student at University of Wisconsin-Madison. In March 2023, I got my degree of M.Eng. at Institute of Image Processing and Pattern Recognition, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Jie Yang and Prof. Xiaolin Huang. In June 2020, I got my degree of B.Eng. at Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Danya Yao.

My research interests include representation learning and trustworthy machine learning. My goal is to contribute to more trustworthy and generalizable AI systems in the future.

Sometimes I take pictures using 35mm & 120 films. Film cameras I'm using: Leica M4, Canon New F-1, Hasselblad 500cm, Contax TVS and Canon A-1.
