About me

I am an incoming Ph.D student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, advised by Prof. Chaowei Xiao. In March 2023, I got my degree of M.Eng at Institute of Image Processing and Pattern Recognition, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Jie Yang and Prof. Xiaolin Huang. In June 2020, I got my degree of B.Eng at Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Danya Yao.

My research interests include representation learning and trustworthy machine learning. My goal is to contribute to more trustworthy and generalizable AI systems in the future.
